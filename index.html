Purpose: To serve as a high-level 'reminder' to the key practices to enforce when engaging with External Experts and an insight into the underlying rationale. Systematize best practices to engage with External Experts based on their expertise to support specific business needs.
  • The intention behind Expert engagement should be clearly documented & justified based solely on legitimate business needs and not to build a relationship with them, gain access, influence decision-making, or reward past / future prescribing
  • The Expert engagement must not be used as a way to educate or share information with the expert other than as required for delivery of the services
  • To prevent the misperception of a 'full-time Gilead Expert' or of 'targeting' certain experts, ensure there is sufficient diversity in Experts engaged
  • Initial approach must recognize & consider any relevant internal procedures to remain compliant (i.e., local teams, appropriate approval process)
